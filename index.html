biographie

I started playing Starblast.io around april 2017, for almost 1 years i played alone on my own, then i started to talk with some people on Discord, and from thread to thread i joined the clans ꞨͲȺꞒꝀ, where i had a great time and i personally keep a very good memory, But when Nukephisics left, the clans desoldered, the activity greatly decreased and there was a large number of departures, and I didn't take pleasure anymore in this clans so I left them, following this event I spent about 6 months without clans nis teams, playing with friends. But one day I felt like joining a clan again, so I joined the Inferno Squadron (IS). After that I met LexyDrow in a pro deatmatch, who turned out to be a Frenchman, and after talking with him he invited me to join the FR team. It was a great experience for me, I met some very good people, and made some friends, I also learned to code with the help of Megalodon, and a lot of other things. I'm now developing several modes like SIW (Starblast Invasion War), with Megalodon, and NOK. And SAW (Starblast Asteroid War) with Megalodon, LX, and Hazard.
